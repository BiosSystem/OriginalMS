# Boss Guides

## Zakum

- **Location:** El Nath - Zakum's Altar (Map 280030000)
- **Level Requirement:** Level 50+
- **Squad Requirement:** Minimum 1 player, max 30 players
- **Mechanics:**
  1. **Arm Phase:** All 8 arms (mob IDs 8800003-8800010) must be defeated first.
  2. **Body Phase 1:** Zakum Body 1 (mob 8800000) spawns after all arms die.
  3. **Body Phase 2:** Zakum Body 2 (mob 8800001) spawns after Phase 1.
  4. **Body Phase 3:** Zakum Body 3 (mob 8800002) - final form. Defeat to collect drops and Skill Books!

## Horntail

- **Location:** Minar Forest - Cave of Life (Map 240050500)
- **Level Requirement:** Level 80+
- **Squad Requirement:** Minimum 1 player, max 30 players
- **Mechanics:**
  1. **Left Wing & Right Wing:** Both wings (mob IDs 8810003 and 8810004) must be destroyed.
  2. **Main Body:** Horntail main body (mob ID 8810007) becomes vulnerable once both wings fall.

## Papulatus

- **Location:** Ludibrium - Deep Inside the Clocktower (Map 220080001)
- **Level Requirement:** Level 100+
- **Entry NPC:** Muirhat (NPC 1201005) at Origin of Clocktower
- **Mechanics:**
  1. **Phase 1:** Papulatus Clock (mob ID 8500001) - heavy physical damage and clock attacks.
  2. **Phase 2:** Papulatus (mob ID 8500002) - inner form emerges after clock shell breaks.
